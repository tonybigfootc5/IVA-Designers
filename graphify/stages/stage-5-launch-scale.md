# Stage 5 — Launch, Scale & Monitoring

## Goal

Prepare IVA for production launch, observability, and controlled scaling.

## Deliverables

- Sentry rollout
- PostHog instrumentation
- Redis / queue integration
- Cost alerts and bandwidth monitoring
- Production runbook and incident response notes

## Exit Criteria

- Deployment runbook is complete
- Monitoring ownership is clear
- Launch blockers are closed or explicitly deferred
